The Problem

Our current energy grid struggles to handle renewable energy fluctuations and unpredictable demand, leading to inefficiencies, higher costs, and blackouts. We need a smarter system to optimize energy use and reduce fossil fuel dependency.

Methodology

Using machine learning, we forecast energy demand and optimize renewable sources like wind and solar. Our platform provides a sleek dashboard and API for real-time insights, enhancing grid stability and resource allocation.
